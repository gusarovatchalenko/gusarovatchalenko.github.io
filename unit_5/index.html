About me

Soul Pilates is dedicated to improve your flexibility, balance, coordination and joint mobility. Working on your deep core muscles, the 'Powerhouse', you will develop good posture, endurance and a sense of empowerment to enjoy the everyday tasks of life. Each session, tailored to your skills and goals, will leave you feeling refreshed and energised. Allow your body to adjust and breathe, heal yourself and open your mind to the magic of movement.
